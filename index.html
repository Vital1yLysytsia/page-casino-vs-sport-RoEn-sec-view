Football betting is a recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

At first, football wagers were a form of entertainment. For example, one can mention pari-mutuel — a long-standing system of pools that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Outside the stadiums, fans bought tickets at a minimum price (later tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, where they were cut out);
  • On the cards, fans entered the results of the upcoming “game number one” matches, which they believed were the most likely;
  • The coupons were handed over to a pool collector agent or sent by mail to the organizing company.

A specialized office guaranteed that during the week there would be at least one winner. The lucky ones were determined by the number of points scored among all fans who sent in their cards.

The pari-mutuel system made it possible to earn big money by placing the first football bets. For instance, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned more than 3 million pounds. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that ended in a 2:2 draw.

The pool system was successful until “bookmakers” began to appear in the form in which they exist today. Fans of the “game number one” got the opportunity to place bets with much greater comfort.
